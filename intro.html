What we do

QCP Spices is an eminent firm engaged in Manufacturing and Exporting a wide range of Sri Lankan Spices. Our products are prepared with the help of high quality machines and are in high demand by our clients for their accurate composition, nontoxic nature and high purity. Apart from this, our clients can avail our products at a reasonable price.

We have a team of experienced professionals who hold a rich experience and educational qualifications. Selected by conducting an in-depth recruitment procedure on the basis of their industrial knowledge and experience, our professionals are always ready to meet with requirements of the industry. Further, our professionals have committed themselves to accomplish the exact requirements of our clients in the most efficient manner.

We believe that once a contract is signed it is sacrosanct. We take great pride in delivering the right goods at the right price and at the right time. We understand this to be the definition of quality.

We do not believe that we should say too much about ourselves but would request you to give us an opportunity to be of service to you. Our values and business ethics will get automatically understood by our esteemed buyers from the quality of goods and the related services that we provide.

Our objective is develop our business as partners of our overseas buyers with emphasis on reliability as the key to our relationship.
